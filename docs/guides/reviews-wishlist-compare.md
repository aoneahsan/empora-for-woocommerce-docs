# Reviews, Wishlist & Compare

Three free-core modules that work together to build trust and reduce purchase hesitation.

## Reviews

Extends WooCommerce reviews with richer widgets and **verified-purchase** indicators.

1. Enable **Reviews** under **Empora → Modules**.
2. Configure display options on **Empora → Settings → Reviews** (where the verified badge shows, review summary widgets, etc.).
3. Reviews continue to use WooCommerce's native review/comment system underneath, so existing reviews are preserved.

The verified-purchase indicator marks reviews left by customers who actually bought the product, which measurably increases trust.

## Wishlist

Lets customers save products for later.

1. Enable **Wishlist**.
2. On **Settings → Wishlist**, choose whether guests can use a wishlist (stored per session) or whether an account is required (stored to the user).
3. A "Add to wishlist" control appears on product and listing pages; customers view their list from their account area.

Wishlists are a strong signal for win-back email campaigns — pair with the **Email Automation** premium module to nudge customers about saved items.

## Compare List

Side-by-side product comparison.

1. Enable **Compare List**.
2. On **Settings → Compare**, pick which fields appear in the comparison table (price, attributes, stock, rating, …).
3. Customers add products to a compare tray and open a comparison view.

Keep the compared fields focused on the attributes that actually differentiate your products.

## Putting them together

A common high-converting setup:

- **Reviews** with verified badges on every product page.
- **Wishlist** enabled for logged-in users, feeding a "still thinking about these?" email.
- **Compare** limited to the 4–6 attributes shoppers care about.
