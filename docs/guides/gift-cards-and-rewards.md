# Gift Cards & Rewards

These **premium** modules add loyalty and stored-value mechanics. They require an active license that includes them — see [Activating your license](/getting-started/activating-your-license).

## Gift Cards

Sell and redeem store gift cards.

1. Enable **Gift Cards** under **Empora → Modules**.
2. Configure on **Settings → Gift Cards**: denominations or custom amounts, delivery (email), expiry policy, and which products count as gift cards.
3. Customers buy a gift card like any product; the recipient redeems the code at checkout.

Gift cards are stored value — keep their balances and redemption rules clear, and decide your expiry policy in line with local regulations.

## Rewards Points

Points-based loyalty: customers earn points for actions (purchases, reviews, sign-up) and redeem them for discounts.

1. Enable **Rewards Points**.
2. On **Settings → Rewards Points**, set earn rates (points per currency unit), redemption value (points → discount), and any caps.
3. Points balances appear in the customer's account; redemption applies at checkout.

Tune earn/redeem rates so the program is attractive but margin-safe. Start conservative; you can always increase generosity.

## Store Credit / Wallet

An account balance customers can top up and spend.

- **Store Credit** is often issued for returns/refunds (pairs with the **Returns/RMA** module).
- **Wallet** lets customers pre-load funds and check out faster.

Enable the one that matches your model; both surface a balance in the customer account and apply at checkout.

## Combining them

A mature loyalty stack:

- **Rewards Points** for everyday earning.
- **Gift Cards** for gifting and acquisition.
- **Store Credit/Wallet** for returns and pre-paid balances.
- **Email Automation** to remind customers of their balances and points.
