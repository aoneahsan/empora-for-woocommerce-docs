# Premium Modules

Premium modules require an active license whose plan includes them. Enable each one under **Empora → Modules** after [activating your license](/getting-started/activating-your-license).

The headline premium modules:

## Advanced Shipping

Weight-, destination-, and tier-based rates plus complex shipping logic that goes well beyond WooCommerce's built-in flat/free rates. Pairs with **Table-Rate Shipping** and **Shipping Rules** for fine-grained control, and **Shipment Tracking** for post-purchase visibility.

## Payments Hub

Additional payment gateways and **Buy-Now-Pay-Later (BNPL)** integrations, with smart routing rules. A dedicated **Square** integration is available as its own module.

## Taxes

Automated tax calculation and reporting helpers (basic and advanced), reducing manual tax table upkeep.

## Inventory

Advanced stock management: low-stock alerts, multi-warehouse stock, and tighter control over how stock is tracked and displayed.

## Bulk Edit

Productivity tooling for editing many products or orders at once — change prices, stock, attributes, or statuses across a selection instead of one item at a time.

## Import / Export

CSV/XML data migration for products and related data — move catalogues in and out, or sync with external systems.

## Reports

Extended analytics beyond WooCommerce's defaults: orders-by-day, top products, refund summaries, dashboard widgets, and **scheduled email reports** so you get trends without logging in.

## Selling models

| Module | What it adds |
|---|---|
| **Subscriptions** | Recurring billing with a customer self-service My Account view (active plans, next billing date, manage links). |
| **Bookings** | Time/resource-based bookings. |
| **Rentals** | Rent products for a period. |
| **Auctions** | Bid-based selling. |
| **Pre-Orders** | Sell before stock arrives. |
| **Quotes** | Request-for-quote flows for B2B. |
| **Digital Downloads** | Enhanced digital product delivery. |

## Conversion & loyalty

| Module | What it adds |
|---|---|
| **Gift Cards** | Sell and redeem store gift cards. |
| **Rewards Points** | Points-based loyalty. |
| **Store Credit / Wallet** | Account balance customers can spend. |
| **Smart Coupons** | Advanced coupon logic. |
| **Waitlist / Stock Alerts** | Back-in-stock notifications. |
| **Abandoned Cart** | Recover incomplete checkouts. |
| **Referral Program / Affiliates** | Word-of-mouth growth. |
| **Popups** | On-site campaigns with A/B testing. |

## Channels & marketing

Email Automation, SMS (and SMS Advanced), WhatsApp (and WhatsApp Advanced), **Klaviyo**, **Instagram Shop**, **Google Listings & Ads**, **Google Shopping Feed**, Social Login. These connect your store to the channels where customers already are.

## Multi-store & B2B

Multi-Currency, Wholesale pricing, Membership, Vendor Management (multi-vendor marketplaces), Custom Fields, Store Locator.

:::tip
The exact modules your license unlocks are listed live on **Empora → Settings → License**. If a module you expect is hidden, confirm it is part of your plan there first.
:::
